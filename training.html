Profile and Training

Background

I am currently in my last year at INSA Toulouse, specialized in Innovative Smart Systems (ISS). Initially, I was doing a Systems Control and Electronic Degree, and then I specialized in Embedded Systems. For my last year, I chose the ISS branch which mixes students from Computer Sciences and Networks, Systems Control and Electronic and Physics degrees. This orientation is an opportunity for me to broaden my range of competences to other domains.

Teaching Program

Course Content Hours
Smart Devices Introduction to sensors and data acquisition, introduction to microcontroller architecture and open source hardware. 61.7
Microcontrollers and Open-Source Hardware (M&OSH)
Communication for IoT Understand the major development phases for mobile communications and development of the associated technology, understand and master optimisation of communication protocols with respect to security and energy concerns. Security for IoT networks, introduction to the impact of exploiting a security vulnerability in an IoT architecture. 63.75
Middleware and Service Training Definition and use of a Service Oriented architecture, deploy SOA with web services, work with Agile (Scrum) method. Know how to situate the main standards for the IoT and deploy an architecture. Understand concept of cloud computing, deploy and adapt a cloud-based platform for IoT. 62
Analysis and data processing, business applications Software engineering, processing semantic data and data processing and analysis (Big Data). 37.5
Innovative Project Conception of a flight simulator architecture (harware & software). Analysis of client needs, suggestion of technological solution to a problem, implement the prototype, produce a report. 80.5
Innovation and humanity Project management, understand production, validation, distribution, acceptability and aftermath of innovation. Learn teamwork, multi-disciplinary students work as a team. 85
