This website provides an overview of my work. This document was realized during the 5th year ISS. It is a targeted collection of the projects I drove and the knowledge I acquired during my studies, added to an analysis of the skills developped as I am about to start my engineer career.

Generalities

First you will find the sections Profile & Training and Resume, which provide generalities about my profile, my training and an overview of my resume both in French and English.

Descriptive Part

This section provides an overview of my different experiences around embedded systems and smart devices. You will find it under the name Experiences. The internship and projects presented in this part highlight the knowledge and skills I acquired during the training. The purpose of this descriptive part is to understand in what context I have evolved and how the development of my skills within this particular context has occurred.

Technical Part

During my journey, I was confronted with problem situations (technical problem solving, strategic choices ...) during which I had to implement a relevant practice to achieve a desired result. I describe such situations in the section named Technical knowledge. I made an exhaustive analysis of all the knowledge and skills I acquired during these different experiences, under a matrix format. This section can be foud under Skills Analysis and General Review.
