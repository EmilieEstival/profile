Skills analysis

This part intends to provide a self evaluation about the ISS training. It is also a way to make an exhaustive analysis of all the knowledge and skills acquired during the different experiences I presented. This assessment will take the forme of skill matrices listing down the skills, learning method and level I reached.

Regarding to learning methods :
IT : Initial Training
PE : Peer Exchange
ST : Self Training
PP : Professional Practice

About the levels :
AP : Application (Follow-up instructions or procedures)
AN : Analysis (Improvement or optimization of solutions or proposals)
P : Proficiency (Program design or specification definitions)
EX : Expert (Definition of orientation or strategies)
